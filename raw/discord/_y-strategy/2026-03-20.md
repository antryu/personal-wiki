So I built a visual orchestration layer. You assign different models to different functions — Qwen for operations, Gemma for content, GPT-4o for risk analysis — and watch them work independently before a synthesis step combines everything.

Works on 8GB laptops. SQLite, zero config. MIT licensed.

What models would you assign to what functions? Curious what combos people would try.
