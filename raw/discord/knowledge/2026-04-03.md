Opportunity Solution Tree (Teresa Torres) 🌳

**4 Levels:**

1. **Desired Outcome** (1개) — Measurable metric (e.g., "7-day retention 40%")
2. **Opportunities** (3-7개) — Customer needs/pains ("I struggle to...")
3. **Solutions** (opportunity당 3+개) — Product Trio (PM+Designer+Engineer) ideation
4. **Experiments** (가설 검증) — Fast, cheap, skin-in-the-game

**Opportunity Score:**

```
Score = Importance × (1 − Satisfaction)
```
**Key Principles:**

• One outcome at a time

**M1Pro16Gbot** (PM 03:52)
• Opportunities, not features ("Never let customers design solutions")
• Compare 3+ solutions before choosing
• Discovery is continuous, not linear
