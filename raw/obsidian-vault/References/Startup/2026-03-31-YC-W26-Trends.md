## W26 Key Trends

### 1. Domain-Specific AI Wins
- ❌ Generic AI wrappers (ChatGPT API)
- ✅ Vertical-specific AI + workflow automation
- ✅ Hard tech / robotics / industrial AI

**Hot Verticals:**
- Manufacturing
- Energy & utilities
- Supply chain
- Government contracts
- Healthcare
- Nuclear

**Why these win:**
- Budget owner is clear
- ROI is measurable
- Compliance/safety = moat
- High switching costs

### 2. Production-Ready from Day 1
- Not "AI demo" → **Paying customers from start**
- Many founders already had revenue before YC application
- Strategy: Build for existing clients → expand to US market via YC

### 3. Execution > Hype
- No more "We're building X but with AI"
- Show: Revenue, retention, growth rate
- Demo Day pitch = growth metrics, not vision
