Howard Marks doesn’t make bets on economic predictions.
That’s especially true now when the biggest wildcard is inflation – a phenomenon no one fully understands. But just because something is unknowable doesn’t mean it’s unimportant. That’s why Howard has devoted his latest
memo to a topic he largely disavows: macro forecasting.
