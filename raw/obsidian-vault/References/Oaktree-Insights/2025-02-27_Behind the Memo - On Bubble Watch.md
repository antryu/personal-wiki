**Date:** 2025-02-27

In the latest episode of *Behind the Memo*,
 Howard discusses his recent memo, *On Bubble Watch*, which revisits a subject he first wrote about 25 years ago. He delves into the investor psychology that underpins a market bubble, and assesses the danger of letting excitement and fear of missing out override risk aversion.
