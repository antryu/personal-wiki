# Awesome Math Resources

> A curated list of awesome mathematics resources, offering a vast array of freely available educational content for learners at all levels.
> Last updated: 2026-05-16

## Overview
The **Awesome Math Resources** repository, hosted on GitHub, compiles a comprehensive list of learning materials, tools, and platforms for studying mathematics. Organized into categories such as general resources, branches of mathematics, and specialized fields like mathematical physics and computer science, this list serves as an essential guide for students, educators, and math enthusiasts. It includes links to online courses, YouTube series, academic textbooks, and open educational platforms, all aimed at making mathematics accessible and engaging.

## Key Points
- Curated list of mathematics resources for free access.
- Covers a wide range of topics from foundational math to advanced areas like topology and logic.
- Designed for diverse audiences, including students, educators, and researchers.
