# China Textbook Repository

> A GitHub repository providing free access to primary, secondary, and university-level Chinese textbooks in PDF format. It aims to promote universal education and bridge educational disparities for domestic and overseas users.  
> Last updated: 2026-04-11

## Overview  
**Purpose**: Centralized open-source distribution of Chinese educational materials to combat restricted access and support overseas Chinese communities.  
**Repository Highlights**:  
- **URL**: [https://github.com/TapXWorld/ChinaTextbook](https://github.com/TapXWorld/ChinaTextbook)  
- **Popularity**: 67.7k stars, 15.1k forks, and 103 open issues as of 2026-04-11.  
- **Content**:  
  - **Primary (K-6) Mathematics**: Textbooks for all grades (e.g., [1st Grade](https://github.com/TapXWorld/ChinaTextbook/blob/master/%E5%B0%8F%E5%AD%A6/%E6%95%B0%E5%AD%A6/%E4%BA%BA%E6%95%99%E7%89%88/%E4%B9%89%E5%8A%A1%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2%E6%95%99%E7%A7%91%E4%B9%A6%20%C2%B7%20%E6%95%B0%E5%AD%A6%E4%B8%80%E5%B9%B4%E7%BA%A7%E4%B8%8A%E5%86%8C.pdf)).  
  - **Secondary (Middle School) Mathematics**: Resources like [7th Grade](https://github.com/TapXWorld/ChinaTextbook/blob/master/%E5%88%9D%E4%B8%AD/%E6%95%B0%E5%AD%A6/%E4%BA%BA%E6%95%99%E7%89%88-%E4%BA%BA%E6%B0%91%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2%E5%87%BA%E7%89%88%E7%A4%BE/%E4%B8%83%E5%B9%B4%E7%BA%A7/%E4%B9%89%E5%8A%A1%E6%95%99%E8%82%B2%E6%95%99%E7%A7%91%E4%B9%A6%C2%B7%E6%95%B0%E5%AD%A6%E4%B8%83%E5%B9%B4%E7%BA%A7%E4%B8%8A%E5%86%8C.pdf).  
- **Motivation**: Addresses private watermarking of resources on platforms and supports educational equity for underprivileged regions and overseas Chinese families.  

## Key Points  
- **Open Access**: Eliminates financial and geographic barriers to quality education materials.  
- **Community-Driven**: High star/fork count reflects widespread demand and trust.  
- **Cultural Bridge**: Aims to preserve Chinese educational continuity for diaspora communities.
