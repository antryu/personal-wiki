# PaddleOCR: Advanced OCR & Document AI Toolkit

> PaddleOCR is a powerful, lightweight OCR toolkit that bridges the gap between images and PDFs and LLMs, enabling users to convert any document into structured data. It supports over 100 languages and is widely used in AI and document processing workflows.
> Last updated: 2026-05-08

## Overview
PaddleOCR is a global leading OCR toolkit and document AI engine developed by PaddlePaddle. It enables users to extract structured data from PDFs and images for use in AI and data analysis workflows. The project is maintained on GitHub and has a large community of users and contributors.

Key features include:
- **Multi-language Support**: Over 100 languages.
- **Document Parsing & Translation**: Extracts and translates text from documents.
- **Lightweight Architecture**: Optimized for performance and scalability.
- **Integration with AI Workflows**: Designed to work seamlessly with LLMs and RAG pipelines.
- **Cross-Platform Support**: Works on Linux, Windows, and macOS.
