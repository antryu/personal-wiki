# Awesome Design Systems

> A collection of awesome design systems, this repository curates a comprehensive list of design systems across various industries and platforms. It's notable for its community-driven approach and its role in helping developers and designers find and adopt best practices.
> Last updated: 2026-04-11

## Overview
The **Awesome Design Systems** repository, maintained by alexpate, is a community-driven collection of design systems from companies and organizations around the world. It includes UI libraries, pattern libraries, and other resources that support design consistency and collaboration. The repository is regularly updated and encourages contributions from the public. Each entry is categorized with tags such as "Components," "Voice & Tone," "Designers Kit," and "Source Code" to help users quickly identify what each design system offers.

## Key Points
- Community-driven curation of design systems.
- Supports both developers and designers with categorized resources.
- Encourages open contributions and collaboration.
