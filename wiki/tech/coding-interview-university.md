# Coding Interview University

> "Coding Interview University" is a comprehensive study plan designed to prepare individuals for technical interviews at top software companies like Amazon, Google, Facebook, and Microsoft. It offers a curated list of resources and topics to help aspiring software engineers build the skills necessary to succeed in technical interviews.
> Last updated: 2026-05-05

## Overview

**Coding Interview University** was originally conceived as a short to-do list for studying computer science topics, but it has evolved into a comprehensive and detailed resource. The project is open-source and maintained under a Creative Commons license, allowing for wide accessibility and adaptation. It is hosted on GitHub and has gained widespread popularity, with over 345,000 stars and 82,000 forks.

This study plan is based on the personal experience of the creator, who successfully landed a job as a Software Development Engineer at Amazon. It outlines a structured learning path that includes programming fundamentals, algorithms, data structures, systems design, and more. The creator emphasizes that while they spent months preparing, the plan is flexible and optimized to avoid unnecessary study time.

The repository contains a detailed README with a table of contents and sections on essential topics, including practice problems and interview tips. It also offers translations in multiple languages and supports community contributions through open issues and pull requests.

## Key Points

- Offers a structured, multi-month study plan for technical interview preparation in software engineering.
- Includes curated resources for data structures, algorithms, systems design, and coding practices.
- Based on real-world success, including the creator's experience landing a job at Amazon.
- Open source and free to use under the Creative Commons Attribution Share-Alike 4.0 International license.
- Maintains translations in over a dozen languages and is actively updated.
