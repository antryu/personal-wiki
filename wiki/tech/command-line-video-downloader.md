# Command-Line Video Downloader

> A powerful and feature-rich command-line tool for downloading videos and audio from thousands of websites. It is a maintained fork of the popular youtube-dl project and is notable for its active development and support for modern platforms and features.  
> Last updated: 2026-04-12

## Overview
yt-dlp is a feature-rich command-line audio/video downloader with support for [thousands of sites](supportedsites.md). The project is a fork of [youtube-dl](https://github.com/ytdl-org/youtube-dl) based on the now inactive [youtube-dlc](https://github.com/blackjack4494/yt-dlc). It offers a wide array of options for downloading, formatting, and post-processing media files, making it a versatile tool for users who prefer command-line interfaces.

The repository has seen significant adoption, with over 156,420 stars and 12,864 forks as of 2026-04-12. It is developed in Python and released under The Unlicense. The project is actively maintained and regularly updated, with a strong community and a wealth of documentation.

yt-dlp supports a variety of features, including:
- Downloading videos and audio from thousands of websites.
- Customizable output templates and format selection.
- Post-processing options such as converting files, extracting audio, and removing video.
- SponsorBlock integration for skipping ads and sponsored segments.
- Support for authentication, geo-restriction bypassing, and network customization.
- Embedding in other applications and scripting for automation.

The project also provides extensive documentation and a vibrant community through platforms like Discord and GitHub discussions.

## Key Points
- Active fork of youtube-dl with improved maintenance and modernization.
- Supports thousands of websites and offers extensive customization options.
- Integrates SponsorBlock for ad-free video downloads.
