# PyTorch Image Models Collection

> The largest collection of PyTorch image encoders / backbones, featuring a wide range of models such as ResNet, ViT, ConvNeXt, and more. It includes training, evaluation, inference, and export scripts along with pretrained weights, making it a go-to resource for image classification and deep learning tasks.
> Last updated: 2026-05-13

## Overview

The **`huggingface/pytorch-image-models`** repository is a comprehensive collection of PyTorch-based image encoder models and tools for image classification, training, and inference. It supports a variety of popular architectures, including ResNet, ResNeXT, EfficientNet, Vision Transformer (ViT), MobileNet, and more, with a focus on flexibility and performance.

Key features include:

- Pretrained weights for various models.
- Scripts for training, validation, and inference.
- Support for multiple hardware configurations and PyTorch versions.
- A wide range of research and production-ready models.

## Key Points

- Offers the largest collection of PyTorch image backbones and encoders.
- Includes training, validation, and inference scripts.
- Provides detailed performance benchmarks across modern GPUs.
- Supports a variety of advanced optimization techniques and training methods.
