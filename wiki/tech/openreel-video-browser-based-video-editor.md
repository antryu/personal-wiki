# OpenReel Video - Browser-Based Video Editor

> OpenReel Video is a professional-grade, browser-based video editor that provides CapCut-like features without the need for installation, cloud uploads, or watermarks. It offers a powerful, open-source editing experience entirely within the browser.
> Last updated: 2026-05-07

## Overview

OpenReel Video is a fully-featured browser-based video editor that operates entirely on the client-side. It is designed for users who want professional-grade editing capabilities without relying on cloud processing or expensive software. Built with React, TypeScript, WebCodecs, and WebGPU, it enables frame-accurate editing, GPU-accelerated rendering, and real-time previews of up to 4K video. The project is open-source and MIT-licensed, ensuring long-term freedom from subscriptions or watermarks.

The editor includes a wide range of video, audio, and graphic tools. Users can edit on multiple tracks, apply keyframe animations, perform color grading, and export in high-quality formats like MP4, WebM, and ProRes. Additionally, OpenReel supports GPU-accelerated workflows and includes advanced features like audio effects, AI upscaling, and custom export settings.

The project was launched on January 19, 2026, and has quickly gained traction with over 1,600 stars and 246 forks on GitHub. It is currently in beta and maintained by the open-source community.

## Key Points

- 100% client-side operation with no cloud uploads or installations required.
- Professional tools for multi-track editing, color grading, and audio mixing.
- Supports GPU acceleration for smooth 4K editing and fast exports.
- Offers a wide range of export formats and customization options.
- Open source and MIT-licensed, ensuring long-term accessibility and privacy.
