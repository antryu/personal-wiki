# Vellum Assistant - Personal AI Assistant

> Vellum Assistant is a personal AI assistant that adapts to the user, offering memory, personality, and proactive communication features. It operates seamlessly across macOS, Telegram, and Slack, making it a versatile and notable AI solution.
> Last updated: 2026-05-09

## Overview
Vellum Assistant is a personal AI assistant built to evolve with the user, integrating features such as memory retention, identity customization, proactive communication, and robust security. Developed as an open-source project, it utilizes TypeScript and the MIT License, offering flexibility and transparency to its users. With support for platforms like macOS, Telegram, and Slack, it provides a centralized AI experience tailored to individual needs. The project has gained traction with 328 stars and 40 forks since its launch on 2026-02-07, and is actively maintained with 117 open issues. Vellum Assistant is notable for its hybrid retrieval system, memory isolation, and sandboxed execution of tools.

## Key Points
- Vellum Assistant learns how users work and adapts to their communication style and preferences.
- It offers proactive communication, reaching out with reminders or updates when needed.
- Security is a core feature, with a fail-closed design and sandboxed execution of tools.
