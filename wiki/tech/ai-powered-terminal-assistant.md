# AI-Powered Terminal Assistant

> This repository, "Forge," is an AI-enhanced terminal development environment that integrates AI capabilities into your coding workflow. It supports over 300 AI models and offers interactive, one-shot, and ZSH plugin modes, making it a versatile tool for developers. The project is notable for its robust features, open-source nature, and growing community.
> Last updated: 2026-04-10

## Overview
The **AI-Powered Terminal Assistant** project, known as **Forge**, is an innovative tool designed to enhance the coding experience by integrating AI capabilities directly into the terminal. Developed in Rust and licensed under the Apache License 2.0, it offers developers a seamless way to interact with AI models like Claude, GPT, Grok, and many others. Forge supports multiple modes of operation, including an interactive TUI, one-shot CLI, and ZSH plugin mode, enabling users to choose the workflow that best fits their needs.

Forge is notable for its flexibility, allowing developers to perform code understanding, implement new features, debug issues, and manage their projects with the help of AI. The project has garnered significant attention, with over 6,400 stars and 1,327 forks on GitHub, and it is actively maintained with updates as recent as April 10, 2026.

## Key Points
- Forge supports over 300 AI models, including popular ones like Claude, GPT, Grok, and Gemini.
- It offers three modes of operation: Interactive TUI, One-Shot CLI, and ZSH Plugin Mode.
- Forge is open-source and built in Rust, ensuring performance and reliability.
- The project has a strong community with active development and over 113 open issues for collaboration.
