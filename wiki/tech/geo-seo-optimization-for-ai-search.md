# GEO-SEO Optimization for AI Search

> This repository provides a comprehensive skill for Claude Code to optimize websites for both AI and traditional SEO, with features like citability scoring, brand authority analysis, and platform-specific optimization. Notable for its integration with AI search engines, it helps marketers adapt to the rapid shift in traffic patterns from traditional to AI-referred sources.
> Last updated: 2026-05-14

## Overview
The **geo-seo-claude** skill is a powerful tool designed to optimize websites for AI-powered search engines such as ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews. It goes beyond traditional SEO by incorporating GEO (Geo-Optimized SEO) strategies, which are essential for maximizing visibility and traffic in an era where AI-driven search is rapidly becoming the dominant force.

The tool includes 13 specialized sub-skills that analyze and optimize various aspects of a website, including citability, crawler access, brand presence, and schema markup. It also generates professional reports in both text and PDF formats, making it ideal for use by marketers and businesses aiming to stay ahead in the evolving digital landscape.

The repository has gained significant popularity, with over 7,200 stars and 1,190 forks, and is open-sourced under the MIT License. It is actively maintained and updated, with the latest revision on May 14, 2026.

## Key Points
- Integrates AI and traditional SEO for optimized website visibility.
- Provides citability scoring and AI crawler analysis to improve discoverability.
- Focuses on brand authority, schema markup, and platform-specific optimization.
- Generates comprehensive reports in both text and PDF formats.
- Uses a modular architecture with 13 specialized sub-skills for targeted optimization.
