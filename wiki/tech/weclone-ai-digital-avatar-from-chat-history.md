# WeClone: AI Digital Avatar from Chat History

> WeClone is a one-stop solution for creating AI-powered digital avatars using chat history. It fine-tunes large language models (LLMs) with your chat logs to capture your unique personality, then deploys them via chatbots to bring your digital self to life. The project has gained significant attention with 17,822 stars on GitHub as of May 2026.  
> Last updated: 2026-05-07

## Overview
WeClone allows users to create a personalized AI "twin" by training a large language model with their chat history. This includes support for text, images, and data from platforms like Telegram. The system provides an end-to-end workflow from data export, preprocessing, model training, to deployment. Users can interact with their digital self via supported platforms, including Telegram and WeChat.

WeClone is open-source and licensed under the GNU Affero General Public License v3.0. It is actively developed and offers documentation and community support through various channels like Telegram and HelloGitHub.

## Key Points
- One-stop solution for creating AI-powered digital avatars from chat history.
- Supports fine-tuning LLMs with chat logs, including images and multimodal data.
- Deployment available on platforms like Telegram, WeChat, Discord, and Slack.
- Prioritizes privacy with localized fine-tuning and data filtering.
