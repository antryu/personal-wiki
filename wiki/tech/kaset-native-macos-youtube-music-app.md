# Kaset: Native macOS YouTube Music App

> Kaset is a native macOS client for YouTube Music built with Swift and SwiftUI, offering a feature-rich and Apple Music-inspired experience. It includes system-level integration, haptic feedback, and Apple Intelligence AI features.  
> Last updated: 2026-05-04

## Overview

Kaset is an open-source macOS application designed to provide a native and polished experience for YouTube Music users. Unlike the web-based or third-party alternatives, Kaset offers deep macOS integration with features like Control Center playback, media key support, and system-wide equalizer. It supports YouTube Music Premium and includes a sleek UI with Liquid Glass effects, making it feel like a first-party Apple app.

The app features robust functionality such as localized support, a powerful search system, track lyrics with AI explanations, and system-wide background audio playback. Kaset also provides extensive customization and extensibility through AppleScript, a custom URL scheme, and WebKit extensions. It supports DRM-protected content and offers advanced controls for managing playlists, podcasts, and playback queues.

Kaset is maintained by contributors and is available as a downloadable release or via Homebrew. As an unofficial app, it is not affiliated with Google or YouTube, and users are advised to comply with YouTube Music's terms of service.

## Key Points
- Native macOS YouTube Music client with Apple Music-style UI
- Full YouTube Music Premium support with DRM playback
- Deep system integration (Control Center, media keys, Dock menu)
- Apple Intelligence AI features for natural language commands and lyrics analysis
- Customizable UI and system-wide equalizer with Spotify-style presets
- Supports WebKit extensions, AppleScript, and a custom URL scheme
- Available in multiple languages and works with macOS 26.0 or later
- Open-source with a permissive MIT license
