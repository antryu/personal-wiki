# Speech Swift: On-device AI Speech Toolkit for Apple Silicon

> Speech Swift is an on-device AI speech toolkit for Apple Silicon, enabling speech recognition, synthesis, and understanding locally without relying on cloud services. It leverages MLX and CoreML for ASR, TTS, speech-to-speech, VAD, and diarization.  
> Last updated: 2026-05-10

## Overview
Speech Swift is a powerful on-device AI speech toolkit optimized for Apple Silicon hardware, offering a comprehensive suite of speech processing capabilities. It supports a variety of functionalities such as automatic speech recognition (ASR), text-to-speech (TTS), speech-to-speech, voice activity detection (VAD), and speaker diarization. The toolkit is built using MLX Swift and CoreML, enabling efficient execution on Apple devices without the need for internet connectivity, API keys, or data transmission beyond the device.

The project provides multiple models for different use cases, including Qwen3-ASR for automatic speech recognition in 52 languages, CosyVoice TTS for voice cloning and multi-speaker dialogue, and DeepFilterNet3 for real-time noise suppression. It also supports advanced features such as speaker embeddings for identifying speakers and real-time dictation with end-of-utterance detection. With support for many languages and a wide range of audio processing tools, Speech Swift is ideal for developers looking to build speech-enabled applications locally on macOS and iOS.

## Key Points
- Fully on-device speech processing with no data leaving the user's device.
- Supports Apple Silicon and integrates with MLX and CoreML for high performance.
- Offers a wide range of features including ASR, TTS, VAD, speaker diarization, noise suppression, and translation.
- Includes models for many languages, including support for 1,672 languages in the Omnilingual ASR.
- Provides tools for both real-time and long-form audio processing, such as real-time dictation and 90-minute podcast synthesis.
