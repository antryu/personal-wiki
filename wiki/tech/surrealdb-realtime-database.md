## Key Points
- Combines **document and graph database** capabilities in a single system, enabling complex queries across structured and interconnected data.  
- Built for **realtime collaboration**, supporting live updates and synchronization across distributed systems.  
- Implements a **serverless architecture** with auto-scaling, making it suitable for IoT, web apps, and cloud-native environments.  
- Active ecosystem with **multi-language SDKs** and integrations for Docker, CI/CD, and package managers (npm, PyPI, NuGet, etc.).  
- Maintains a **highly engaged community** through Discord, social media, and developer platforms.
