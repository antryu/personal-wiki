# Dify - Agentic Workflow Platform

> Dify is a production-ready platform that enables the development of agentic workflows by offering a low-code/no-code solution for integrating and orchestrating AI agents. It stands out for its flexibility and support for various AI models and frameworks.
> Last updated: 2026-05-07

## Overview
Dify is a powerful tool for developers and businesses looking to build and deploy AI-driven workflows. It provides an intuitive interface for designing, testing, and managing agentic workflows without requiring deep programming expertise. The platform supports multiple AI models, including OpenAI, Gemini, and others, and allows users to implement custom logic, RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation), and orchestration capabilities. It also offers self-hosting options for organizations seeking more control over their AI workflows.

Key features include support for multiple AI providers, low-code/no-code workflow design, self-hosting, and integration with popular frameworks and tools. Dify is actively maintained with regular updates and a strong community presence across Discord, Reddit, and social media.

## Key Points
- Offers a low-code/no-code platform for building agentic workflows.
- Supports integration with multiple AI models and providers such as OpenAI, Gemini, and GPT-4.
- Enables orchestration of AI agents with custom logic and RAG capabilities.
- Provides both cloud-based and self-hosting options.
- Has a large community and active development, with over 140,000 stars on GitHub.
- Includes multilingual documentation and community support across Discord, Reddit, and Twitter.
