# NVIDIA NeMo: Generative AI Framework for LLMs and Speech AI

> NVIDIA NeMo is a scalable generative AI framework designed for researchers and developers working on Large Language Models (LLMs), multimodal AI, and Speech AI, including Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) and Text-to-Speech (TTS). The project has become a go-to toolkit for speech AI research and production, with strong community support and active development.
> Last updated: 2026-05-15

## Overview
NVIDIA NeMo is a powerful open-source AI toolkit developed by NVIDIA, focused on building and deploying generative AI models, especially in speech and language domains. The framework supports both research and production workflows and is known for its flexibility, scalability, and performance across a wide range of tasks.

The project has evolved significantly since its creation in 2019, and with an active development cycle and large community (17k+ stars on GitHub), it continues to push the boundaries of speech and language AI. In 2026, the repository is undergoing a major transformation, focusing specifically on speech, audio, and multimodal LLMs, while prior versions support a broader set of modalities.

NeMo offers pre-trained and fine-tuned models for a variety of use cases, including ASR, TTS, speech translation, and voice chat. These models are available through HuggingFace and NVIDIA's Build platform, making it easy for developers to deploy and experiment with state-of-the-art AI capabilities.

## Key Points
- NeMo supports both research and production environments for speech and language AI.
- The framework includes pre-trained models for ASR, TTS, speech translation, and voice chat.
- NVIDIA NeMo is open source under the Apache License 2.0 and is written in Python.
- The project is actively maintained, with a GitHub repository updated as of 2026-05-15.
- Major updates in 2026 include the release of NeMo Speech and a transition to focus on audio and speech LLMs.
