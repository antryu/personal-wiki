# Local AI Fine-Tuning on MacBook with Gemma 4

> Gemma 4 Fine-Tuner enables multimodal AI fine-tuning on a single MacBook, demonstrating advancements in local AI development. This approach allows developers to optimize models using consumer-grade hardware without cloud infrastructure.  
> Last updated: 2026-04-10

## Overview  
The article discusses the use of **Gemma 4 Fine-Tuner**, a tool that facilitates **local multimodal AI fine-tuning** on a MacBook. This method eliminates the need for cloud-based resources, making AI model customization accessible for individual developers and small teams. The process leverages consumer-grade hardware to train and adapt AI models for specific tasks, highlighting the efficiency and democratization of AI development in 2026. The work is attributed to 박재홍 (Park Jae-hong) and shared via Google's internal link-sharing platform.

## Key Points  
- Gemma 4 Fine-Tuner enables **local multimodal fine-tuning** on MacBooks, bypassing cloud infrastructure requirements.  
- The tool demonstrates how **consumer-grade hardware** can handle complex AI training tasks, reducing costs and increasing accessibility.
