# Academic Research Skills for Claude Code

> A comprehensive suite of Claude Code skills for academic research, covering the full pipeline from research to publication. This notable project emphasizes human-AI collaboration to avoid pitfalls of full automation in academic work.
> Last updated: 2026-05-14

## Overview

The **Imbad0202/academic-research-skills** repository provides an AI-powered tool designed to assist academic researchers in all stages of the research and writing process using the Claude Code platform. It supports tasks like literature review, writing, peer review, and finalization. The tool is built around the principle that AI should act as a supportive copilot rather than an autonomous writer, focusing on improving the quality and integrity of research work.

Key features include:

- **Socratic dialogue-based planning** (`/ars-plan`) to help researchers outline their papers.
- **Literature review generation** (`/ars-lit-review`) for topic-based searches.
- **Style calibration** to adapt to the user's writing voice.
- **Writing quality checks** to reduce machine-generated patterns.
- **Integrity gates** that check for common AI research pitfalls (e.g., citation hallucinations, methodology fabrication).
- **Integration with tools like Semantic Scholar API, VLM for figure verification, and APA 7.0 formatting**.

The project is inspired by research into AI research systems and emphasizes the importance of human-in-the-loop design, referencing studies such as Lu et al. (2026) and PaperOrchestra (2026) to ensure a robust and ethical approach to AI-assisted research.

## Key Points

- **Human-in-the-loop design**: AI supports but doesn't replace human decisions, improving quality and reducing errors.
- **End-to-end research pipeline**: Covers research planning, writing, review, and finalization.
- **Quality assurance mechanisms**: Includes 7-mode integrity checks and opt-in calibration using user-supplied gold data.
- **Integration with academic tools**: Supports Semantic Scholar API, citation management, and format compatibility (APA, DOCX, PDF).
- **Open-source and extensible**: Designed for use with Claude Code CLI, VS Code, and JetBrains.
