# SadTalker: Audio-Driven Talking Face Animation

> SadTalker is a deep learning model developed for generating realistic talking face animations driven by audio. It uses a single input image and audio to produce high-quality talking head videos. The model was presented at CVPR 2023 and is notable for its ability to produce natural facial expressions and movements.
> Last updated: 2026-05-10

## Overview

SadTalker is a repository for audio-driven talking face animation, developed by researchers from Xi'an Jiaotong University, Tencent AI Lab, and Ant Group. The system learns realistic 3D motion coefficients from audio signals to animate a single image into a talking face video. It was introduced in the CVPR 2023 paper and has since gained attention in the research and open-source communities.

## Key Points

- Uses audio to animate a single face image into a realistic talking head video.
- Implements 3D motion coefficient learning for natural facial movements.
- Features an updated Apache 2.0 license and is available for commercial and non-commercial use.
- Offers full-body and image-generation modes in addition to face animation.
- Integrated into Discord for easy access and text-to-video generation.
- Includes a Stable Diffusion Web UI extension for enhanced usage.
