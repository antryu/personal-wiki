# Odoo Open Source Business Apps

> Odoo is a suite of web-based open source business apps designed to help businesses manage and grow. It offers a range of integrated modules such as CRM, accounting, inventory, and more, making it a powerful ERP system. The project is notable for its modular architecture, active community, and extensive customization options.
> Last updated: 2026-05-13

## Overview

Odoo is an open source software that provides a comprehensive set of business applications, all built around a modular architecture. These applications can be used independently or together to create a full-featured ERP system. The platform supports a wide range of business functions including customer relationship management (CRM), website building, e-commerce, warehouse management, project management, billing and accounting, point of sale, human resources, marketing, and manufacturing.

The project has a large and active community with over 50,631 stars and 32,394 forks on GitHub. It is written in Python and licensed under a non-standard "Other" license. The repository was created on May 13, 2014, and has seen consistent development and updates since.

Getting started with Odoo is straightforward, with detailed documentation and setup instructions provided. Developers and businesses can choose from multiple learning resources, including eLearning modules and business simulation games. Odoo also offers a robust security framework and encourages responsible disclosure of vulnerabilities through its dedicated security page.

## Key Points
- Provides a full-featured open source ERP system.
- Modular and customizable to meet the needs of various business functions.
- Active and growing open source community with extensive support and resources.
