# Easydict: macOS Dictionary & Translator App

> A concise and elegant macOS dictionary and translator app that supports multiple translation services and features like OCR screenshot translation and automatic language recognition. It's notable for its wide range of supported services and user-friendly design.  
> Last updated: 2026-04-19

## Overview
Easydict is a macOS application designed for quick and efficient word lookup and text translation. It is built with Swift and released under the GNU General Public License v3.0. The app supports a variety of translation services, including Apple Dictionary, Apple Translate, OpenAI, Gemini, DeepL, Google, Bing, Tencent, Baidu, and many others. It features OCR screenshot translation, automatic language detection, and support for 48 languages, making it a versatile tool for users who need quick translations.

The app has gained popularity since its launch in November 2022, with over 12,900 stars and 636 forks on GitHub. It is continuously updated and maintained, with the latest update occurring in April 2026.

## Key Points
- Supports over 20 translation services, including Apple Dictionary, Apple Translate, OpenAI, and DeepL.
- Features OCR screenshot translation and automatic language recognition.
- Offers multiple TTS voice services and supports 48 languages.
- Open-source and licensed under the GNU General Public License v3.0.
- Actively maintained with a large community and extensive documentation.
