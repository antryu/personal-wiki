# JobOps: DevOps for Job Hunting

> JobOps applies DevOps principles to job hunting, offering a self-hosted pipeline to track, analyze, and assist your job application process. It streamlines job searches across multiple platforms, tailors resumes, scores job fits, and tracks applications in one centralized dashboard.  
> Last updated: 2026-04-18

## Overview

JobOps is a powerful, open-source tool that brings DevOps efficiency to the job hunting process. It enables users to search across 10+ job boards from a single interface, automatically tailor their CV to each role, and track their applications in real-time. By combining automation with AI-driven insights, it boosts productivity without sacrificing the personal touch required by recruiters.

JobOps is notable for its comprehensive feature set, ease of self-hosting, and growing popularity on GitHub. It is particularly praised for its refusal to auto-apply, which avoids the risk of being flagged as spam. Instead, it focuses on enhancing the speed and quality of job applications.

The tool is developed in TypeScript and is self-hostable via Docker, making it accessible to both developers and non-technical users. It has a vibrant community and is actively maintained, with 34 open issues as of the latest update.

## Key Points

- **One-stop job search**: Search multiple job boards from one unified dashboard.
- **AI-driven CV tailoring**: Automatically rewrites your resume to match each job description.
- **Post-application tracking**: Integrates with Gmail to automatically detect and update application statuses.
- **Self-hosted and secure**: Designed to be self-hosted, ensuring data privacy and control.
- **No auto-applying**: Avoids the risk of blacklisting by not automating the actual application process.
