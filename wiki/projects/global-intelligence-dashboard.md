# Global Intelligence Dashboard

> Real-time global intelligence dashboard combining AI-powered news aggregation, geopolitical monitoring, and infrastructure tracking in a unified situational awareness interface. Notable for its 435+ curated news feeds, dual 3D/2D map engine, and cross-domain risk analysis capabilities.  
> Last updated: 2026-04-18

## Overview
**koala73/worldmonitor** is an open-source platform offering real-time global intelligence through AI-driven synthesis of news, geopolitical events, and economic indicators. The repository features:
- **Repository Metrics**: 48.9k stars, 8.0k forks, and 118 open issues as of April 2026
- **Technical Stack**: Built with TypeScript, utilizing globe.gl and deck.gl for dual 3D/2D mapping
- **Deployment**: Available as web apps ([worldmonitor.app](https://worldmonitor.app)) and native downloads for Windows, macOS, and Linux
- **Licensing**: AGPL v3 license with active development since January 2026

The dashboard includes specialized variants for finance, commodities, and general monitoring, supported by 45 data layers and a Country Intelligence Index that aggregates 12 risk categories. A community Discord server and extensive documentation support user contributions.

![World Monitor Dashboard](docs/images/worldmonitor-7-mar-2026.jpg)

## Key Points
- Aggregates and AI-synthesizes 435+ news feeds across 15 categories into actionable briefs
- Dual-map visualization system combining 3D globe and WebGL flat map with 45 interactive data layers
- Cross-domain correlation of military, economic, and disaster signals to predict escalation risks
- Finance tracking module monitors 92 stock exchanges, commodities, and cryptocurrencies
- Open-source architecture with AGPL v3 license and active community contributions
