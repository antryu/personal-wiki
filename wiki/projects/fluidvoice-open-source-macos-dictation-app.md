# FluidVoice: Open-Source macOS Dictation App

> FluidVoice is a fast, open-source voice-to-text dictation app for macOS that offers fully local, offline transcription with support for multiple AI models. It's notable for its real-time performance, low latency, and compatibility with Apple Silicon.  
> Last updated: 2026-04-10

## Overview

FluidVoice is a macOS application designed for voice-to-text transcription with AI-powered enhancements. It supports a variety of speech recognition models, including Parakeet Flash, Parakeet TDT v3/v2, Cohere Transcribe, Apple Speech, and Whisper. The app allows users to transcribe speech in real-time with low latency and offers two primary modes: **Command Mode** for executing actions on the Mac and **Write Mode** for typing directly into any application.

The project is open-source and released under the GNU General Public License v3.0. It is written in Swift and optimized for Apple Silicon, with fallback support for Intel Macs using Whisper. The latest release includes a new **Parakeet Flash** model for real-time English dictation and an expanded voice engine lineup. 

The app features a global hotkey for instant voice capture, live transcription preview, menu bar integration, and auto-updates. It also includes a stats panel to monitor usage and an opt-in beta channel for early access to new features.

## Key Points

- Fully **open-source** and **offline-first** voice-to-text dictation app for macOS.
- Supports **multiple AI models** for transcription including Parakeet, Cohere, Apple Speech, and Whisper.
- Offers **real-time transcription** with low-latency performance, especially on Apple Silicon.
- Includes **Command Mode** and **Write Mode** for executing actions and typing in any application.
- Features **AI enhancement** through integration with providers like OpenAI and Groq.
- Available for **Apple Silicon** and **Intel Macs** with model-specific compatibility.
- Actively maintained with over **1,700 stars** and a growing feature set.
