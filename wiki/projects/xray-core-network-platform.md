# Xray-core Network Platform

> Xray-core is a powerful and flexible open-source network platform designed to provide enhanced privacy and secure communication. It supports multiple protocols, making it a robust solution for bypassing censorship and ensuring secure internet access. It is notable for being the best v2ray-core alternative and for its extensive support for modern and experimental protocols like REALITY and XHTTP.  
> Last updated: 2026-04-19

## Overview

Xray-core is part of Project X, originating from the XTLS protocol. It is an open platform that offers a suite of tools for network communication, including support for various proxy protocols such as VMess, VLESS, Trojan, Shadowsocks, and more. The platform is designed to be modular, extensible, and compatible with a wide range of use cases, from personal privacy to enterprise-grade secure tunneling.
